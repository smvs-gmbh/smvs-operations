#Vision

SMVS Operations is the central operational platform of SMVS for monitoring, analysing and managing Swiss NMVS operational activities. The platform consolidates operational data from multiple NMVS-related sources and provides a foundation for AI-assisted analysis, alert management and operational intelligence.

#Scope
##Scope v1

* Exceptions Monitoring
* Snapshot Monitoring
* Product Intelligence
* Organisation Intelligence
* Alert Intelligence
* Import Framework
* Audit Logging

##Out of Scope v1

* Direct modifications in NMVS
* Automated alert closure
* VerifyIt integration
* AMS Hub integration
