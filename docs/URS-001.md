User Requirements Specification
===============================

#Core

##URS-001
The system shall provide a central operational platform for SMVS.

##URS-002
The system shall support role-based access control.

##URS-003
The system shall maintain an audit trail of relevant user and system actions.


#Data Integration

##URS-010
The system shall import operational data from NMVS-related data sources.

##URS-011
The system shall support scheduled and manual data imports.

##URS-012
The system shall maintain a history of imported data.

#Exceptions

##URS-020
The system shall provide visibility of NMVS exceptions.

##URS-021
The system shall support searching and filtering exceptions.

##URS-022
The system shall store exception audit trail information.


#Alters

##URS-030
The system shall provide alert intelligence capabilities.

##URS-031
The system shall correlate alerts with related operational information.

##URS-032
The system shall support future AI-assisted alert analysis.

#Products
##URS-040
The system shall provide product intelligence capabilities.

##URS-041
The system shall associate NMVS operational events with product information.

#Future AI

##URS-090
The system shall support AI-assisted operational analysis.

##URS-091
The system shall support AI-generated recommendations.

##URS-092
The system shall ensure that AI recommendations are distinguishable from user actions.
